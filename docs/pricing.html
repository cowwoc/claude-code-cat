Frequently Asked Questions

Can I use Indie for commercial projects?

Yes! Indie is free for solo developers regardless of whether your project is commercial or personal. You only need Team when you want collaboration features.

What happens if I exceed my seat limit?

Additional users will see core Indie functionality but won't have access to Team features until you add more seats or an existing seat is released.

Is there a free trial for Team?

Yes, Team includes a 14-day free trial with full access to all collaboration features. No credit card required to start.

What's the billing cycle?

Team can be billed monthly or annually (annual saves 2 months). Enterprise is billed annually with volume discounts available.

Can I switch plans anytime?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade at any time. When upgrading, you'll be prorated. When downgrading, the change takes effect at your next billing cycle.

What about offline usage?

CAT includes a 30-day grace period for license validation, so CI/CD pipelines and air-gapped environments work without interruption.

Ready to ship on schedule?

Start with Indie for free. Upgrade when your team grows.
